Tic-Tac-Toe Game

Game rules

Tic-tac-toe is a two-player game played on a 3x3 grid. Players take turns marking spaces with 'X' or 'O'. The first player to get three of their symbols in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins. If all spaces are filled and no one has three in a row, the game is a draw.
